Why Join?

Tactile Learning

The 4-Day Sprint

Day 01

Discovery & Ideation

Break down the brief. Learn to brainstorm without limits and identify the core hook of your campaign.

Day 02

Strategy & Planning

Map out the execution. Who is the audience? What is the medium? Build the blueprint.

Day 03

Content Creation

Shoot, write, design. Turn the blueprint into tangible assets ready for the real world.

Day 04

Pitch & Execution

Present your campaign. Defend your choices. Launch the project and review the impact.

Your Outcomes

  • A Real Portfolio Piece: Walk away with a completed campaign to show future clients or employers.

  • Creative Confidence: Learn to trust your instincts and articulate your creative vision clearly.

  • The Network: Connect with a cohort of driven, like-minded students and mentors.
